About



The pursuit of knowledge has always been hampered due to lack of resources, equipment, or technical know-how. But in this digital age, these limitations can easily be tackled through the use of Internet. Virtual labs gives the power to access, facilitate, enhance, and improve the process of learning and experimentation without being bounded by geographical distances or logistic deficiency.
Web-shared resources and experiments can be used to stimulate research and induce an atmosphere of innovation in students. This collective knowledge of researchers, professors, and students can be highly useful for providing distance learning to every computer. This not only exposes the students to Practical implementations of engineering concepts but also aids them in their academic or research work.
